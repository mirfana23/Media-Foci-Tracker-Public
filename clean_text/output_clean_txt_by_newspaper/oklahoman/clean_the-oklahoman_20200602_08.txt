The coronavirus pandemic has hit many businesses hard, including media companies.
The parent company of The Oklahoman has instituted one-week, unpaid furloughs for all of its full-time employees each month f rom April through June.
Local journalism is more important now than ever.
Please consider subscribing at checkout.oklahoman.com.
Here is a list of newsroom employees who are out this week: state/county government reporter Kayla Branch; political reporter Chris Casteel; executive editor Kelly Dyer Fry; online editor Richard Hall; photo editor Doug Hoke; entertainment editor Brandy McDonnell; real estate editor Richard Mize; copy editor Glen Seeber; sports columnist Berry Tramel and courts reporter Tim Willert.
