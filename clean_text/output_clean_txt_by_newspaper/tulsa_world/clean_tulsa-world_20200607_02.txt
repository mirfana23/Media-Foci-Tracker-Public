Guard arrested after fatal shooting
Tulsa World - 7 Jun 2020 - 1 - By Kelly Hines and Samantha Vicent
A security guard at an east Tulsa motel was arrested Saturday after he fatally shot another man, police said.
Christopher Straight, 53, was booked into the Tulsa County jail on a complaint of first-degree manslaughter and was released on bond Saturday evening.
