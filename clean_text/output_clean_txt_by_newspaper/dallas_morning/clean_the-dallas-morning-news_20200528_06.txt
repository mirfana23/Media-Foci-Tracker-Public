Poll safety
The Dallas Morning News - 28 May 2020 - 1A
Secretary of State Ruth Hughs offered an advisory Tuesday that included recommendations for polling places: Make hand sanitizer, disinfecting wipes or other disinfectants readily available.
Immediately disinfect any item that comes into contact with a voter.
Have polling place workers sanitize their hands after interacting with voters or other workers.
Consider face coverings for poll workers.
Place 6foot separation markings on the floor.
