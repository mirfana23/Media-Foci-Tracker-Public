The average price for a dozen eggs
The Dallas Morning News - 13 Jun 2020 - 1A
Egg prices are volatile anyway, but the price spiked in April as consumers cooked and baked more at home during the pandemic.
Prices are for a dozen Grade A eggs in major cities in the southern U.S., including Texas.
